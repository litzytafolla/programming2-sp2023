Gluten-Free Quiche

Ingredients

  • Pie crust (GF crust)
  • Half-and-half or Milk
  • Four large eggs
  • Salt and pepper (seasoning 🧂)
  • Favorite quiche toppings (bacon, tomato, onion, cheese, etc.

Steps to make GF Quiche egg base

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees fahrenheit or 176 degrees celsius
  2. In a medium bowl, whisk eggs
  3. Add half-and-half(or milk) and seasoning into the eggs

GF Quiche base

  1. Spread your pie crust on the botton of your pie pan
  2. Make a layer of bacon
  3. Add a layer of cheese
  4. Add a sprinkle of onion and tomato
  5. Pour the cheese mixture into the pie pan
  6. Sprinkle half a cup of cheese on top

Baking the Quiche

  1. Put your quiche in the oven
  2. bake for 20 minutes
  3. Take out your quiche and cover loosely with a layer of aluminum foil
  4. Place the quiche back in the oven for 25 minutes
  5. Let the quiche cool for 10 minutes
  6. Serve! 😋🍽️
