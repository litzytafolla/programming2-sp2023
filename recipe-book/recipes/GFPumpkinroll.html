Gluten-Free Pumpkin rolls

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1 cup granulated sugar
  • 2/3 cup canned pumpkin puree
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1 tsp pumpkin pie spice
  • 1/2 tsp cinammon
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 3/4 cup all-purpose GF flour
  • 1/8 cup powdered sugar

Ingredients for creamcheese frosting

  • 8-oz package cream cheese, softened
  • 6tbs butter, softened
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • 1 tsp pure vanilla extract

GF Cream Cheese frosting

  1. In a medium-sized bowl, beat the butter, cream cheese, and pure vanilla extract together until smooth
  2. Add in the powdered sugar and mix together until smooth

Steps to make GF Pumpkin rolls

  1. Preheat oven to 375¬∞F. Line a 15 x 10-inch jelly-roll pan with parchment paper
  2. Spray the parchment paper with gluten-free cooking oil spray
  3. Beat the eggs and granulated sugar until fully combined
  4. Add the pumpkin pure, baking powder, baking soda, salt, pumpkin pie spice, cinnamon, and the pure vanilla extract to the egg mixture and mix until fully combined
  5. Add in the gluten-free all purpose flour and mix until fully combined
  6. Spread the batter evenly into prepared pan
  7. Bake for 13 to 15 minutes
  8. Once the cake is out of the oven, immediately cover the baking pan with aluminum foil
  9. Allow cake to cool
  10. Once the cake is cooled to room temperature, remove aluminum foil
  11. With a spatula, spread cream cheese frosting over cake
  12. Turn the cake so that one of the short edges of the cake pan is closest to you. Using the parchment paper, lift the edge of the cake closest to you (the short edge) and gently fold it over itself to start the roll
  13. Gently lift the parchment paper to continue the roll the cake away from you until the cake is in a log shape
  14. Wrap the gluten-free pumpkin roll in plastic wrap and refrigerate at least one hour. Sprinkle the pumpkin roll with powdered sugar before slicing.
