Gluten-Free Crabcakes

Ingredients

  • One large egg
  • 1/4 Cup of mayonnaise
  • 2 tsp Gf Dijion mustard
  • 2 tsp GF Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp Old Bay seasoning
  • 1/4 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp lemon juice ğŸ‹
  • 1 tbs chopped fresh parsley
  • 3/4 cup gluten-free bread crumbs
  • 1lb lump crab meat
  • Butter

Steps to make GF Crabcakes

  1. Whisk together egg, mayonnaise, mustard, and Worcestershire sauce, Old Bay, lemon juice, and salt
  2. Add parsley, bread crumps, and the crab meat on top
  3. Very gently fold the together the ingredients (try not to break up the crab meat)
  4. Cover the mixture with plastic wrap and place in refridgerator for 30 min (or up to 24 hours)
  5. Spray the baking sheet with GF cooking spray
  6. Portion the crab cake mixture in 1/2 cup increments (do not flatten them)
  7. Use your hands or a spoon to compact each crab cake

GF Crabcake baking

  1. Preheat oven to 450 degrees fahreinheit (232 degrees celsius)
  2. Lightly brush each crabcake with melted butter
  3. Bake for 15-20 minutes
  4. Let cool
